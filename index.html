My work

Hello world!

I’m a web developer with a passion for solving problems and creating interactive experiences with code, creativity and my co-workers.

I was born and raised in a small Swedish town called Piteå. Now I reside in Uppsala, where I’m studying my last semester for a bachelor’s degree in Computer & Information Science at Uppsala University.

My interest for web development started in high school and has escalated rapidly ever since. Through my six years of experience I’ve worked as a developer in web projects, both big and small, as a teacher in web design and on numerous hobby projects. All of which have presented interesting and challenging problems to solve, but most importantly, endless hours of fun!

I specialize in front end development and always do my best to stay up-to-date with the latest frameworks and techniques for optimizing the quality and productivity of my work. I also have experience working with various back end languages such as ASP.NET, C#, Node.js, PHP and SQL.

The name “go diagonal” originates from an expression used by the electronica duo Boards of Canada and basically means to ”think outside the box”. This is a central principle in all my work. I believe that creative thinking, attention to detail and exchange of knowledge are the keys to taking big ideas to the next level.

Besides crunching code I keep myself busy with playing guitar in a band, taking photos of people and places, brewing beer with friends and the occasional video game. I'm also very interested in Japanese culture and I've spent two years of my life living in Tokyo and studying the Japanese language.
