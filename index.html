The name “go diagonal” originates from an expression used by the electronica duo Boards of Canada. Whenever one of their tracks felt to standardized one of them would shout ”go diagonal!” and the other one would have to do something to the track that went diagonally over the path the song was taking. Thinking outside the box, in other words. I try to apply this principle to all of my work in order to find creative solutions and make it more interesting. Minus the shouting.

Besides crunching code I keep myself busy with playing guitar in a band, taking photos of people and places, brewing beer with friends and the occasional video game.
